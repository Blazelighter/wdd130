Genres

Knowing what you like

Genre Name

Platformers: A genre based around the maneuvering of the character through an environment.

Examples:

  1. Mario Bros.
  2. Sonic
  3. Celeste

Fighting Game

A game genre defined by competitive player vs. player 1 on 1 gameplay.

Examples:

  1. Street Fighter
  2. Tekken
  3. Guilty Gear

Metroidvania

A style of game which largely focuses on exploring an interconnected maze-like world, while gaining abilities that allow fur further exploration. The term itself comes from the two game series Metroid and Castlevania, which are some of the earliest examples of this style of game.

Examples:

  1. Metroid
  2. Hollow Knight
  3. Ori and the Blind Forest
